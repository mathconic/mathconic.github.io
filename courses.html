Courses

The courses are designed to cater to the unique needs of each student, ensuring a deep understanding of mathematical concepts and problem-solving skills. While the customized plan is always available depending on the student's need; however, as a general rule, the courses are intended to cover all the topics for CBSE, AICBSE and various state boards.

Class IX and X

  • Number Systems (Real Numbers)
  • Algebra (Polynomials, Linear Equations, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic Progression)
  • Coordinate Geometry
  • Geometry (Lines, Angles, Triangles, Quadrilaterals, Circle)
  • Mensuration (Areas and Volumes)
  • Trigonometry
  • Probability and Statistics

Class XI and XII

  • Sets, Relations and Functions
  • Trigonometric Functions, Inverse Trig. functions
  • Complex Numbers and Quadratic Equations
  • Algebra (P&C, Binomial Theorem, Sequences and Series)
  • Coordinate Geometry (St. Lines, Conic Sections)
  • Matrices and Determinants
  • Differential and Integral Calculus
  • Application of Derivatives and Integrals
  • Differential Equations
  • Vectors and 3-D Geometry
  • Probability and Statistics

About the Tutor

I hold a double-postgraduate degree, including one in Mathematics, and I am an alumnus of the University of Delhi. My teaching focuses on helping students understand the why behind every concept before learning the how. Mathematics is not merely a collection of formulas and procedures—it is a way of thinking. My approach to teaching is built on the belief that every student can develop confidence in mathematics when concepts are presented with clarity, patience, and logical progression.

Each lesson is conducted in a one-to-one setting, allowing the pace, depth, and teaching style to be tailored to the individual student's needs. The objective is not only to achieve excellent academic results but also to build a strong mathematical foundation that serves students well in higher studies and beyond. I believe that genuine understanding creates confidence, and confidence leads to lasting success.

How it works

  • Step 1: Enrollment
    • You contact me through Contact Us page.
    • We have an online discussion for around half an hour for initial assessment of the student and creating a personalized plan.
    • You proceed with the payment, at least for one session in advance.
  • Step 2: Tutoring Sessions
    • Online classes are held through modern teaching methodologies and advanced mathematical tools.
  • Step 3: Continuous Support
    • Regular assignments at the end of each class.
    • Periodic feedback shared with parents/guardians.
